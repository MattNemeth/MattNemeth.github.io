Cricket GUI

I have always enjoyed the game of darts. There is something about the game being so simple yet so difficult that I enjoy. My roommates and I always keep score in our heads since we do not have a chalkboard so I decided I would try and make some sort of GUI to keep track of score. There are many applications and websites that already do this but I wanted to try my hand at making my own.
While it is not the prettiest thing in the world, it functions. And that is what is important to me. There are many bells and whistles I plan to add but for now it works for what we need.

Experience

L3 Technologies - Space and Sensors Sector, Intern

Overview

I have spent my first three co-op semesters at L3 Techonologies located in Mason, Ohio. Each semester I was a part of their Digital Design group working on the logic inside of their FPGA's for their cameras.

Responsibilites

  • Submit Change notices and ensure that all relevant documentation is up to date
  • Develop reusable, generic VHDL synchronizers
  • Created a schematic from a port list
  • Collaborated with other engineers to ensure obsolete parts on our PCBs had working replacements

What I Learned

  • The importance of being attentitive to details
  • Creating a plan instead of jumping right in can save enormous amounts of time
  • Books are important! While the internet is extremely useful, books are also a very valuable resource
  • Digital Design is not for me!
